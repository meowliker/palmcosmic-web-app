2. Palm Image Data and AI Processing

PalmCosmic uses artificial intelligence to analyze palm images you upload. This section explains how we handle this sensitive data.

What We Collect

  • Photographs of your palm(s) that you voluntarily upload for analysis
  • AI-generated analysis data derived from your palm images, including interpretations related to life path, relationships, career, and other palmistry insights

How Palm Images Are Processed

  • Palm images are processed by our AI systems to generate palmistry readings
  • Analysis is performed to identify palm lines, mounts, and other features relevant to palmistry interpretation
  • Our AI guide, Elysia, uses this analysis to provide interactive, personalized readings

‚úì What We Do NOT Do

  • We do NOT use palm images for biometric identification purposes
  • We do NOT share raw palm images with third parties for advertising
  • We do NOT use palm images to identify you personally outside of palmistry
  • We do NOT sell palm image data

Data Retention for Palm Images

  • Palm images and associated readings are stored to maintain your reading history
  • You may request deletion of your palm images and readings at any time
  • Upon account deletion, all palm images and readings are permanently removed
